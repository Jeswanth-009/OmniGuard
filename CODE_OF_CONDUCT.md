# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be respectful, inclusive, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and professional in discussions.
- Assume good intent.
- Give actionable, respectful feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing, threats, or intimidation.
- Spam or deliberate disruption.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, lock discussions, or ban contributors for violations.
